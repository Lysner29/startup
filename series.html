Select a series to browse through the available titles.

Living Composers Series

Associated with our ever-growing on-line Living Composers Catalog, this series features the works of some of the most talented of today's composers. We feel that among these works you will find some of tomorrow's classics. Play something new today!

Hard to Find Music Series

A new feature for us, but one that is sure to grow in the future, this series was created to help you find music to play for those hard-to-find combinations of instruments. Browse through the works we already have available, or if we don't have what you're looking for yet, let us know what you need and we'll do our best to find it for you and add it to our catalog. In some cases, we'll even go as far as to commission a composer to write a new work for your instrumentation.

New Versions of Old Favorites Series

Looking for a new solo to play, but your chief instrument is tuba? We might have what you're looking for. This new and growing series features new arrangements of works you may have heard before. Got a special request? We'll do our best to get it for you.

Legacy Series

Looking for something that's been around for a while? Our aim is to bring you highest quality performance editions of those works that have built the legacy we enjoy today.

Large Ensembles Series

Our growing collection of compositions for large ensembles. This series includes works for large wind ensemble, concert band, orchestra, brass choir and other combinations. If your performing group has 15 or more players, this is the place to look for something new.

Choral Music Series

A collection of fine arrangements and compositions for choir. Included are works for both religious and secular settings.

Education Series (Coming soon)

Quest Haven Publishing is pleased to announce that we will soon be publishing a variety of texts, exercises and other materials for the young music student. Check here soon for further details.
