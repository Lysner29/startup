About Us

Performers

We want to help you as a performer meet your needs. To us, this means more than simply having a catalog of works available for you to purchase. At Quest Haven, we listen to your input, and then we shape our services and products around the things you tell us that you need.

One of our main goals is to provide resources that will bring today's composers and performers together. The Living Composers Catalog and our Commissions and Competitions events are designed to give you access to the cutting edge of today's music. We urge you to take advantage of these resources and welcome any suggestions or requests that you might have.
